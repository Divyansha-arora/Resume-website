Reasons I'm amazing

Being creatively curious, I pursued my Bachelor’s degree in Architecture from the University School of Architecture and Planning, Indraprastha University. This curriculum combined my theoretical knowledge with practical abilities in critical thinking, space design, and consumer requirements. Moreover, five years of undergraduate education expanded my horizons to integrate design and profit maximization with the best interest of all the parties associated with the project of building a living space. Academically, studio classes such as Design Studio helped me gain creative insights and problem-solving skills.

For as long as I have dabbled in the world of optimal usability, the one thing that I’ve learned that must always remain supreme is the meaningfulness and relevance of visceral emotions and how a space is reflective of human behavior in design. In this regard, as an architect I wear many hats– designer, visionary, psychologist and analyst, truly understanding the dimensions of a project and possessing a creative mindset to think about how things can be explained, interpreted and utilized. These theoretical underpinnings were a direct result of the courses I undertook during my graduate degree. Moreover, my preference was highly weighted towards generating high-quality products based on feedback."
